## Description

English:

Classic Music Journey is a free listening app for discovering classical composers through music, short introductions, historical portraits, and poetry.

Each composer page pairs a famous public-domain classical theme with a matching public-domain poem. In Chinese mode, the app uses classical Chinese poetry. In English mode, it uses public-domain Western poetry excerpts. As the synthesized music plays, the poem gradually appears, creating an immersive listening and thinking experience.

Features:
- 30 bilingual composer themes
- English and Simplified Chinese interface
- Synthesized public-domain classical melodies
- Composer portraits or elegant color placeholders
- Matching poem reveal during playback
- Swipe left or right to move between composers
- Free, offline-first, no account, no ads, no tracking

Simplified Chinese:

《古典音乐之旅》是一款免费的古典音乐聆听 app，用音乐、短介绍、历史肖像与诗歌帮助用户认识作曲家。

每个音乐家页面都会把一段经典公版主题旋律与一首契合的公版诗歌搭配在一起。中文模式使用中国古诗，英文模式使用西方公版诗歌节选。随着合成音乐播放，诗句会逐渐浮现，形成沉浸式的音乐与思维体验。

功能：

- 音乐家肖像或优雅主题占位图

- 免费、离线优先、无需账号、无广告、无追踪

## Review Notes

The app is free and offline-first. It does not collect user data, does not use analytics or advertising SDKs, and does not require an account. Music is generated from synthesized piano-style note sequences based on public-domain compositions and piano arrangements; no commercial sound recordings are bundled.
