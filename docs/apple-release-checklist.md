# Apple Release Checklist

Use this checklist before App Store Connect submission.

## App Review Readiness

- Test the app on a real device and current iOS simulator.
- Confirm there are no crashes, placeholder screens, broken links, or unfinished content.
- Publish the support/privacy/audit page on `https://jamesyyy.com/classic-music-rhythm/`.
- Use the `jamesyyy.com` page as both the Support URL and Privacy Policy URL unless separate pages are created.
- Confirm the App Store privacy details say no data is collected, if the app remains offline and analytics-free.
- Confirm the app is listed as free with no in-app purchases.
- Confirm English and Simplified Chinese copy are reviewed before screenshot generation.
- Review `docs/theme-catalog.md` and approve the 30 prepared themes.
- Prepare screenshots showing composer intro, music playback, gradual poem reveal, and swipe navigation.

## Intellectual Property

- Keep the first version on synthesized public-domain compositions.
- Do not bundle commercial recordings unless there is written license documentation.
- Document any future audio/image source and license in this repo before submission.
- Review `docs/asset-credits.md` before submission and keep it aligned with bundled app assets.

## Preview Gate

The owner must preview and approve:

- App running on device or simulator.
- App icon and screenshots.
- App Store name, subtitle, description, keywords, and category.
- `jamesyyy.com` support/privacy/audit page.
- China market filing plan.
