# China ICP / App Market Checklist

Mainland China app distribution usually requires mobile app filing (移动互联网应用程序备案), tied to the app operator, domain, server/provider, and app identity.

## Before China Market Submission

1. Confirm the legal operator for the app.
2. Confirm whether `jamesyyy.com` has an ICP filing and whether the app pages will be hosted on mainland China infrastructure.
3. Prepare app name, package/bundle ID, service category, screenshots, privacy policy, operator credentials, contact details, and domain details.
4. Complete required mobile app filing through the hosting/provider or local app-market-required process.
5. Add the filing number to the public web page if required.
6. Keep the app content aligned with its educational/classical music introduction purpose.

## App Design Choices That Help

- No user-generated content.
- No account system.
- No payments.
- No ads or tracking SDKs.
- No external streaming, comments, chat, or social sharing in the first release.

These choices reduce review and compliance surface area for both Apple and China app markets.
