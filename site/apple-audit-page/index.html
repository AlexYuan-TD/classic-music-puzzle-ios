Classic Music Rhythm

Support, privacy, and app review information for the free iOS classical music rhythm app.

App Purpose

Classic Music Rhythm introduces famous classical composers through short educational pages, inspiring messages, synthesized public-domain musical themes, and a piano-key rhythm game with flowing staff notation and a cute dinosaur key guide.

Pricing

The app is free. It has no in-app purchases, subscriptions, paid content, ads, or tracking.

Privacy Policy

Classic Music Rhythm does not collect, store, sell, share, or track personal data. The app does not require an account and does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

If a future version adds optional online features, this page and the App Store privacy details will be updated before release.

Music Rights

The app uses synthesized note sequences based on public-domain classical compositions. It does not bundle commercial sound recordings.
