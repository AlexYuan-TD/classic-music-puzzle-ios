Classic Music Journey

Support, privacy, and app review information for the free iOS classical music listening app.

App Purpose

Classic Music Journey introduces famous classical composers through short educational pages, synthesized public-domain piano themes, and matching public-domain poetry that gradually appears during listening.

Product preview page: Classic Music Journey by James Yuan

About James Yuan

James Yuan loves classical music and is dedicated to introducing it to more listeners. He also follows music technology closely, believing that thoughtful technology can help more people feel the beauty of classical music.

Learn more at www.jamesyyy.com.

Languages

The app supports English and Simplified Chinese content, with an in-app language switcher.

Pricing

The app is free. It has no in-app purchases, subscriptions, paid content, ads, or tracking.

Privacy Policy

Classic Music Journey does not collect, sell, share, or track personal data. The app does not require an account and does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

If a future version adds optional online features, this page and the App Store privacy details will be updated before release.

Music Rights

The app uses synthesized piano-style note sequences based on public-domain classical compositions and piano arrangements. It does not bundle commercial sound recordings.

Poetry Rights

The app pairs music with public-domain poetry. Chinese mode uses classical Chinese poetry, and English mode uses public-domain Western poetry excerpts.
