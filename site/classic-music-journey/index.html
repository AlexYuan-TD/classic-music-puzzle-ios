Classic Music Journey

A free iOS listening app by James Yuan. It introduces classical composers through blurred portraits, famous piano themes, inspiring quotes, public-domain poetry, reflective notes, and a Maestro assistant.

古典音乐之旅是一款免费 iOS 应用，用音乐家肖像、钢琴主题、诗歌、听后感与 Maestro 助手，引导更多人感受古典音乐的美好。

About James Yuan / 关于 James Yuan

James Yuan loves classical music and is dedicated to introducing it to more listeners. He also follows music technology closely, believing that thoughtful technology can help more people feel the beauty of classical music. Learn more at www.jamesyyy.com.

James Yuan 热爱古典音乐，致力于推广古典音乐，同时关注音乐科技。他相信通过有温度的科技，可以让更多人感受古典音乐的美好。了解更多 James Yuan：www.jamesyyy.com。

Support, Privacy, and Rights

Classic Music Journey is free. It has no ads, no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions. For support, contact support@jamesyyy.com.

The app uses synthesized note sequences based on public-domain classical compositions and public-domain poetry excerpts. It does not bundle commercial sound recordings.

Before release to mainland China app markets, the operator will complete required mobile app filing / ICP-related procedures where applicable.
